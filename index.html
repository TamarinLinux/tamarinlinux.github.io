Tamarin Linux

"The truly immutable distro"

Tamarin Linux is a minimal, independent Linux distribution built entirely from source. It is reproducible, transparent, and immutable by design, offering full control without unnecessary abstractions or hidden automation.

Designed for technically curious users and developers, the system is modular, verifiable, and suitable for both servers and desktops.
